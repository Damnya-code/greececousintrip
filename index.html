The shape of the journey

Athens first.
Crete from west to east.

The overnight ferry handles the longest crossing while we sleep. From Chania, the route moves naturally across Crete through Rethymno to Heraklion. No unnecessary return journeys and no full day sacrificed to getting back where we started.

Travel design

The route does
the hard work.

Three well-chosen bases, one overnight crossing and a useful Plan B keep the logistics in the background—where they belong.

01

Choose the right base

Plaka or Koukaki in Athens; Old Town or Stalos in Chania; Lions Square or the port in Heraklion.

02

Sleep through the crossing

A private cabin turns Piraeus to Souda into a hotel night that happens to arrive in Crete.

03

Keep a Plan B

Windy Elafonisi? Switch to Falassarna or a winery. Rough sea to Santorini? Do Knossos, a winery and Agios Nikolaos.
