Day 01 Saturday, 10 October

Athens,
at golden hour.

Arrival, ancient streets and the first dinner together.

Discover ↓

The mood

The journey begins softly: bags down, shutters open, and the Acropolis already visible above the city.

There is no race to collect sights today. Athens is best introduced on foot, moving from sun-warmed ruins to a hilltop sunset and a long table in Psyrri.

An afternoon
that unfolds.

  1. 14:00
    01 / Settle in

    Arrive in the historic centre

    Check into a two-bedroom apartment in Plaka, Monastiraki or Koukaki. Take a breath, claim the balcony, and reset.

    Map ↗
  2. 16:00
    02 / Wander

    Ancient Agora and old-city lanes

    Walk beneath the Acropolis through the Agora, the Temple of Hephaestus and the lively seam between Monastiraki and Plaka.

    Map ↗
  3. 19:00
    03 / Golden hour

    Philopappos Hill at sunset

    Climb while the heat fades. From the ridge, the Parthenon catches the last amber light and the city stretches toward Piraeus.

    Map ↗
  4. 21:00
    04 / Together

    Meze in Psyrri

    Order for the table: grilled cheese, fava, keftedes, seasonal vegetables and the first carafe of local wine.
