The mood

After Santorini, the itinerary comes back down to earth—roughly four thousand years back.

Knossos gives the group painted columns, palace ruins and the Minotaur story before lunch and the first airport goodbye. The rest of the afternoon stays loose and unhurried: beach, café or simply doing very little after six fairly ambitious days.
