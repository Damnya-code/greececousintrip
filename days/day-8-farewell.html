Day 08 Saturday, 17 October 2026 · Heraklion

No rush,
except the flights.

Coffee, one final choice and the road home.

Discover ↓

The mood

The final morning needs coffee, one last look at Crete and enough time to reach the airport without sprinting through it.

Depending on the confirmed flights, choose the beach, Heraklion’s old town or the respectable option of turning breakfast into the main activity. Keep luggage collection and the airport buffer fixed; everything before that can remain pleasantly negotiable.
