Harbour light, Venetian streets and the first day behind the wheel.

Discover ↓

After a night on the ferry, Chania does most of the work: harbour light, narrow Venetian streets, strong coffee and a villa waiting somewhere nearby.

ΧΑΝΙΑ

Start with Chania-style bougatsa: crisp pastry, soft mizithra filling, sugar and cinnamon. Dinner shifts to grilled fish, octopus or shellfish with seasonal greens. Raki usually arrives after the plates are cleared, whether or not anyone remembers ordering it.

Location overview

Souda Port sits east of Chania. Collect the car there, drive into the Old Town, then continue to Marathi only if the afternoon remains free. The return drive puts dinner back in Chania.
