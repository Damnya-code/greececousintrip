  1. 09:00
    01 / Check out

    Map ↗
  2. Morning drive
    02 / Eastbound

    Drive toward Rethymno

    Route ↗
  3. 10:30
    03 / Old Town

    Walk Rethymno’s Venetian lanes

    Enter through the compact historic centre for stone doorways, shaded courtyards and the Rimondi Fountain.

    Map ↗
  4. 11:30
    04 / Above town

    Climb to the Fortezza

    Walk the old citadel walls for views over Rethymno’s rooftops, harbour and the north coast.

    Map ↗
  5. 13:00
    05 / Lunch

    Eat before the second drive

    Choose a taverna in or near the Old Town for Cretan plates, seasonal produce and a properly seated lunch.

    Map ↗
  6. Afternoon
    06 / Continue east

    Drive to Heraklion

    Return to the coastal road for the next leg, usually around an hour without significant delays.

    Route ↗
  7. Around 16:00
    07 / New base

    Check into central Heraklion

    Unload, confirm the parking arrangement and note the walking route to tomorrow’s ferry check-in point.

    Map ↗
  8. 17:30
    08 / Harbour

    Walk to Koules Fortress

    Follow the pier around the Venetian harbour to the sea fortress, with the city walls behind.

    Map ↗
  9. 20:00
    09 / Dinner

    Farm-to-table in Heraklion

    Finish with seasonal Cretan cooking in the centre and keep the evening sensible enough for an early alarm.
