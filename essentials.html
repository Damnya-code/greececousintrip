Useful extras

Travellers arriving from outside Europe should check whether their chargers require a plug adapter and whether the devices support the local power supply.

One person should also remember where the rental car was parked.

Before leaving home

Before the airport.

01

Mobile access

Check roaming coverage or arrange an eSIM before departure.

02

Offline maps

Download Athens, western Crete, Heraklion and Santorini for offline use.

03

Offline documents

Save tickets, accommodation details and important addresses somewhere that does not depend on mobile reception.

04

Final transport check

Reconfirm flight, ferry and transfer timings shortly before departure.

05

Payments

Check that cards work abroad and keep a second payment option available.

06

Entry requirements

Check current official entry requirements shortly before departure, particularly for travellers arriving from Canada and the United States.

07

Group contact

Make sure everyone has the accommodation addresses, arrival plan and a way to contact the group.
